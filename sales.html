Will Smith Music
Will Smith was by no means a niche artist, here I will discuss a little about his sales figures.

His best selling album was "Big Willie Style" which sold 11,650,000 copies worldwide.

His most successful single was "Men in Black", a song written by will for the film of the same name. It went number one in basically every country and went platinum multiple times over.


Will has sold roughly 60 million records at the time of writing, this combines all albums, sales and his work with Jazzy Jeff before he started his solo career.
